1. What actually are Real Numbers?

Well, you have asked a good question! There are different kinds of numbers like Natural Nuumbers, Whole Numbers, Integers, Rational Numbers and irrational numbers and we group all these numbers into one kind or set, namely Real numbers, which are represented by the letter (R).

2. Is 3.14 a real number?

Yes, Obviously it will be because Real Numbers include all the types of numbers no matter if it a fraction or decimal like in this case. Even our so called 'pi' which is a non-terminating and repeating decimal is too a real number.
